HaveAGoodDay? Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

HaveAGoodDay? is designed as a private, local daily mood tracker. The app does not require an account and does not send your ratings or notes to a server.

Information You Enter

The app lets you save a daily mood rating and an optional note about your day. This information is stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device storage.

Data Collection

HaveAGoodDay? does not collect, sell, share, or transmit your personal data. The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or cloud sync.

Notifications

If you choose to allow notifications, HaveAGoodDay? may schedule a local reminder to rate your day. Notification permission is handled by iOS, and reminders are generated on your device.

Data Storage and Deletion

Your mood ratings and notes remain on your device. Deleting the app will delete the data stored by the app from that device.

Children’s Privacy

HaveAGoodDay? does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app’s privacy behavior changes. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support information listed on the App Store product page.
