Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 9, 2026

Clinkd respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when using Clinkd and this support website.

Information We Collect

When you contact support, we collect the email address and message you submit. In the app, Clinkd may collect information you choose to enter, such as session activity, drink entries, display names, and related app interactions.

How We Use Information

We use your information to operate Clinkd, provide support, respond to messages, improve the app, maintain safety, and troubleshoot technical issues.

Support Tickets

If you submit a support ticket through this website, your email and message are stored so we can view your request and reply to you by email.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when needed to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, protect users, or use trusted service providers that help us run Clinkd.

Data Storage

Support messages may be stored using a third-party database provider. App data may be stored using the services that power Clinkd. We take reasonable steps to protect stored information, but no online service can guarantee perfect security.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information associated with your support request or app account, where legally and technically possible.

Age Requirement

Clinkd is intended only for users who are legally allowed to use the app in their location. Do not use Clinkd if you are not legally permitted to do so.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, please contact us through the Clinkd support form.
